A Foreigner Found with drugs at Istanbul Airport

by Anadolu Agency

DIYARBAKIR, Turkey Arts
Oct 04, 2021 10:43 am GMT+3
Arrest at the Airport, Oct. 10, 2021. (AA Photo)
by Anadolu Agency
Oct 04, 2021 10:43 am

A traveler named Andrew Ryan originally from Finland is facing a felony drug charge after investigators said approximately some cocaine was found hidden inside his luggage at Istanbul International Airport.

The Airport police said they were alerted around Sunday evening, when a machine flagged his bags on a flight that arrived from Ercan airport heading to zaventem airport Belgium but on transit in Istanbul turkey at about 7.17 am in the morning on a Sunday during check in..it was gathered that he claimed he didn’t know anything about the said substance recovered after the airport security went through his luggage. Information gotten from a correspondent showed that he has been detained and would charged as required but given few days to get a defend counsel to plead for this bail as he would further be investigated.....
